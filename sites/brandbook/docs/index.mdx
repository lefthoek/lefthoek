# Lefthoek Brandbook

Lefthoek tries to solve the problem of knowledge spill. The fact that a lot of
information in organisations is not stored properly.

One has to practice what one preaches, however. And that's the sole purpose of
this site. It is the place where we collect all the documents that are relevant
for those new to Lefthoek. 

Relevant is a difficult term. That's why I came up with a few guidelines:


###### 1. When in doubt, include it

What is noise for one, may be critical for someone else. 


###### 2. Clean up, Refactor

The fact that a document is for internal use, doesn't mean that anything goes.
