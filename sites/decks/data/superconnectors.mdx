# Middlemen

Offline, many professional challenges are solved by voluntary or involuntary
**facilitators**. Online these roles and people are often lacking. 

</Basic>

---

<Basic>

# Superconnector

Among those middlemen there are a few that really excel at these facilitating
tasks. These superconnectors are essential for creating and maintaining **healthy
circles**: organizations, networks, and ecosystems.

</Basic>

---

<Basic>

# Goal

To use your knowledge, experience, and skills as superconnectors to develop a
**shared online space** for the Dutch tech ecosystem.

</Basic>

# Affinity Mapping

Affinity Mapping is a practice used to organize ideas or insights. It allows
large numbers of ideas stemming from brainstorming to be sorted into groups,
based on their natural relationships, for review and analysis.
