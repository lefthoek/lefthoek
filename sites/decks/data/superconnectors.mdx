<Basic>

# Middlemen

Offline, most professional challenges are succesfully solved by
voluntary or involuntary **facilitators**. Online, people that fulfil these
roles and corresponding tasks are often lacking. 

</Basic>

---

<Basic>

# Super Connector

Among those middlemen there are a few that really excel at these facilitating
tasks. These super connectors are essential for creating and growing **healthy
circles**: organizations, networks, and ecosystems.

</Basic>

---

<Basic>

# Goal

To use your knowledge, experience, and skills as superconnectors to develop a
**connected online space** to raise the Dutch tech ecosystem.

</Basic>
