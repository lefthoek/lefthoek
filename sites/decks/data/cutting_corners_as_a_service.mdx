  # What problem is Lefthoek solving?

</SectionCover>

---

<List>

<dt>Pleasure Drain</dt> 

<dd>The phenomenon that running a company becomes increasingly less fun and
exciting the longer you do it</dd>

</List>

---

<List>

<dt>Founders of Value-Driven Online-First Companies</dt>

<dd>Value-driven is not the same as social or ethical</dd>

<dd>Online-first means that the primary point of contact with customers,
employeees and/or suppliers is through the internet</dd>

<dd>Google, AirBnB, Gusto, Bunq, and <a
href="https://enjoywishlist.com/25-top-workplaces-and-their-core-values/">
some</a> more <a href="https://builtin.com/company-culture/company-core-values-examples">examples</a></dd>

</List>

---

<List>

<dt>Knowledge Spill</dt> 

<dd>Employees constantly share explicit and tacit knowledge
with one another</dd>

<dd>But the tools, and techniques to store, transfer, and archive that
information rarely work</dd>

<dd>Either because of laziness (virtue) or ignorance (vice)</dd>

<dd>As a result, you (the founder) become the tool</dd>

</List>
